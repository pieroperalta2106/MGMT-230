# Team Cloud9 Collective
Erfan – Founder & Creative Director
Erfan is the visionary behind Cloud9 Collective. A Vancouver-based designer and streetwear enthusiast, he handles all creative direction — from concept sketches and graphic design to seasonal collection curation. With years of self-taught experience in Adobe Suite and a deep pulse on West Coast and global streetwear trends, Erfan ensures every piece feels authentic, on-trend, and true to the brand’s “comfort meets confidence” ethos.
Piero – Marketing & Community Lead
Piero runs social media strategy, content creation, and community building. A natural storyteller with a strong eye for aesthetics, he manages Instagram, TikTok, campaigns, influencer outreach, and customer engagement. His background in digital marketing and passion for music and photography helps Cloud9 speak directly to Gen Z in a way that feels genuine and scroll-stopping.
Heer – Operations & E-commerce Manager
Heer keeps everything running smoothly behind the scenes. She oversees the Shopify store, inventory management, order fulfillment, customer service, and supplier relationships. With a sharp attention to detail and prior experience in retail and logistics, Heer makes sure every package arrives on time and every customer has a seamless Cloud9 experience.
Tommy – Business Development & Strategy
Tommy handles the numbers, growth strategy, and future scaling. From financial planning and pricing to pop-up events, partnerships, and wholesale opportunities, he’s focused on turning Cloud9 into a sustainable, recognizable Canadian brand. With a background in entrepreneurship and streetwear resale, Tommy brings the business mindset needed to grow smart and steady.
